Ranks

All the ranks on OCN. (Written by goodbobul)

Donation ranks

Decanus

Decanus

You can buy this badge here. It is $5 for 30 days of perks. It gives you a black badge on the website but no asterisk in game.

Example: bleangamer

Optio

Optio

You can buy this badge here. It is $5 for 30 days of perks. It gives you a black badge on the website but no asterisk in game.

Example: Without_Regret

Centurion

Centurion

You can buy this badge here. This gives you 180 days for $25. It also gives a yellow badge on the website and a yellow asterisk in game. This gives you all of the perks that donors before the donation update got, however, it is only for 180 days.

Example: Magic_Mat

Dux

Dux

You can buy this badge here. It gives you unlimited donor perks for $50 dollars. You get a purple badge on the website and a purple asterisk in game. This gives you all of the perks that donors before the donation update got. It is also unlimited, as the other donors got. However, it is five times the price.

Example: goodbobul

Staff ranks

Administrator

Administrator

An Admin has an orange/yellow badge on the forums and is represented by and in game yellow star. They can ban/warn/kick/freeze people. They can also set the next map on a server. The can also moderate the forums and delete and lock topics. The Admins are "in control" of the mods. We currently have seven admins.

Example: MonsieurApple

Developer

Developer

This is someone who have helped with the coding of the plugin (PGM) or the website. They have a purple badge. They do not get an ingame

Example: Anxuiz

Moderator

Moderator

A moderator Has a red badge on the forums and is represented by a red star in game. They have the abilities of an admin in the game except for the /give commend and they cannot moderate the forums. Project Ares does not accept moderator requests and the admins choose who to mod next and invite them privately. Do not ask for mod as it greatly decreases your chances of getting it.

Example: Beebou

Junior Moderator

Junior Moderator

These are staff that are less experienced than normal moderators. It is basically a stepping stone for new moderators. The Admins will see how mature each one is and decide whether or not to mod them completely. After a two week period, the final decision is made. They can do everything that moderators can do except end/setnext maps.

Example: PlasmaCross
